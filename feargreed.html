What is the Fear & Greed Index?

The Fear & Greed Index is a market sentiment indicator that measures investor emotions and behaviors. It ranges from 0 (Extreme Fear) to 100 (Extreme Greed).

How it works: The index analyzes multiple data points including market volatility, trading volume, social media sentiment, and safe haven demand to gauge whether investors are too fearful or too greedy.

Investment insight: Extreme fear often presents buying opportunities, while extreme greed may signal market tops. Contrarian investors use this as a timing tool.
